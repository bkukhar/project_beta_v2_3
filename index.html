About Me

Hello and welcome!

My name is Irene, I am a self-taught photographer with 2 year experience and just a very creative person. I come from Ukraine, that part of the world where nature is extremely green and beautiful and this is the first source of inspiration I have had from early childhood.

Now interesting people inspire me a lot, this is why I am fond of portraits, emotional ones, those which hide stories behind them. I am sure that only a picture which tells a story may be called a real photo, otherwise that picture is worth nothing. This is a distinctive difference between simple picture taking and professional photography - photographers think over each piece of art they produce and I am no exception. I also have that trait of character you would call either an advantage or a disadvantage, this is up to you to decide - I always chase perfection.

So if you need a person who will take an emotional portrait of you or your family with a story behind it and a hint of perfection you are welcome to contact me.
